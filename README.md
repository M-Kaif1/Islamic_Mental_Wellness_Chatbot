# Islamic Mental Wellness Chatbot

## Overview
The **Islamic Mental Wellness Chatbot** is an interactive Python-based chatbot designed to support mental and spiritual well-being. It provides guidance based on **Quranic verses and Hadith**, offers **mindfulness exercises rooted in Islamic practices**, and helps users reflect on their daily spiritual routines.

The chatbot detects the user's emotional state, provides advice, and encourages mindfulness and connection with Allah through prayer (Salah), remembrance (Dhikr), and reflection (Dua).

This project demonstrates the integration of **AI-inspired interaction, natural language input handling, and spiritual guidance** in a console-based Python application.

---

## Features
- **Emotion Detection:** Recognizes emotions such as stress, anxiety, sadness, tiredness, loneliness, and frustration based on user input.
- **Islamic Tips:** Provides advice rooted in **Quranic verses and authentic Hadith** relevant to the detected emotion.
- **Motivational Quotes:** Offers inspirational quotes from the Quran verses.
- **Mindfulness Exercises:** Recommends practical exercises like dhikr, prayer, and gratitude reflection for calming the mind.
- **Mini Spiritual Reflection Quiz:**  
  - Asks two simple questions:  
    - Did you pray today?  
    - Did you remember Allah (dhikr) today?  
  - If the user answers “yes” to both, the bot responds with a **dua**: *“MashaAllah, may Allah bless you and strengthen your heart.”*
- **Session Tracking:** Tracks mood mentions during the session and provides a summary at the end.
- **Interactive and AI-like:** Allows free-form text input and provides personalized responses based on user mood and requests.

## Limitations

- The chatbot is **console-based** and cannot detect emotions beyond simple keyword matching  
- Limited to **Quran and Hadith references**; does not include other Islamic literature  
- The advice is **general guidance**; it does not replace professional mental health support  
- Mood detection may not always capture **nuanced emotional states**
